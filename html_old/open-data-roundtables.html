2016 Open Data Roundtables

The White House Office of Science and Technology Policy and the Center for Open Data Enterprise will co-host four Open Data Roundtables in 2016.

Roundtable registration is closed.
Visit our Convene page for background materials on each Roundtable. We welcome your comments and suggestions. Please send feedback to Katherine Garcia.


Protecting Privacy - March 24, 2016
How can we open granular information while protecting privacy?


Improving Data Quality - April 27, 2016
What are the most efficient and scalable ways to improve data quality?


Applying Research Data - May 25, 2016
How can we best share and apply government-funded research data?


Public-Private Collaboration - June 15, 2016
How can we collaborate to support open data?
