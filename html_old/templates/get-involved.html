Get Involved

Fellowships and Internships:

The Center for Open Data Enterprise has a limited number of opportunities available for interns and visiting fellows. We welcome applications from people who share our commitment to open data, and who have strong backgrounds in public policy, government, communications, data science, technology, or other relevant areas. Please contact us if you are interested.

Partners

The Center for Open Data Enterprise works with a number of companies and organizations that have a shared interest in advancing demand-driven open data. We welcome opportunities to collaborate with academic, non-profit, and for-profit organizations on projects in the U.S. and internationally. If you are interested in partnering with us, please contact Laura Manley at laura@odenterprise.org

Consulting Engagements

The Center and its staff take on selective consulting engagements for other organizations that support and promote the use of open data. Our work includes strategy, research, roundtables, events, and expert consultation in other areas. Current clients include the World Bank and the new Open Contracting Partnership.
