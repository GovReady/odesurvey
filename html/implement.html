We  Implement.

As an action research center, we apply what we learn from our research to improve open data programs in the U.S. and internationally. To do this, we: 

  • Work with government agencies to improve their data management practices
  • Facilitate the business use of open data for economic growth, business creation, and optimization
  • Conduct sector-based research on the potential for open data

Current Work

U.S. Open Data Transition Report, a nonpartisan report of open data recommendations for the next U.S. presidential administration, to be released in October 2016. Read more about the report here.

Open Data for Business (OD4B) Tool, a methodology to assess the private sector’s current and potential use of government data in various cities, countries, and regions. The Tool, developed in collaboration with the World Bank Group, can be found here.
