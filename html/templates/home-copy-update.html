What is Open Data?

Open data is free, publicly available data that anyone can use and access, without restrictions. Open data can be used by businesses nonprofits, governments and citizens to launch new initiatives and ventures, analyze trends, make data-driven decisions, and solve complex problems.

What We Do

We focus on the demand side of open data for better informed policy making, more targeted solutions, and to maximize return on investments in open data.  Open data is a valuable public resource with a growing number of applications in healthcare, finance, energy, education, agriculture, and other sectors of the economy.
